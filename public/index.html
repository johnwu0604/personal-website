About Me

Born in Newfoundland, raised in Vancouver, and now studying in Montreal. I have lived on both the Pacific and Atlantic coast, while visiting almost every major city in between. Moving around has given me an open mind to try new things and to step out of my comfort zone. By doing so, I have been able to diversify my experiences and create a long profile of knowledge.


I am currently a third year student at McGill University in the process of obtaining my Computer Engineering Degree. Although my expertise revolves around software development, my skills and knowledge expand beyond this technical limit. This ranges from varsity sports and class president in high school to holding several executive positions on student organizations in university. Playing sports has taught me the importance of the determination, discipline, and dedication required to achieve a personal goal. Being a student body representative has given me the natural instinct to make decisions that benefit a whole organization over any individual. Holding positions on different clubs has given me the skills that enable me to adapt to collaborating with different types of people.


I hope this website will allow you to learn more about me. However, if you would ever like to chat more about my experiences or simply have a cup of coffee, feel free to send me an email through my Contact Page.
